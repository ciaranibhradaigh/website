Séimh Counselling & Psychotherapy offers professional, confidential counselling and psychotherapy in Clontarf, North Dublin, for adults, adolescents, and children.

Book a Free Consultation

Located in a convenient and accessible North Dublin setting, with free parking, close to Howth Junction Dart Station and on the N6 bus route. This is a space where you can slow down, feel heard, and explore what’s going on for you in a safe and non-judgmental environment.


If you are experiencing anxiety, stress, low mood, or navigating challenging times, psychotherapy can support you in making sense of your experience and finding a way forward.

About Áine

Every person’s experience is unique. As a pluralistic psychotherapist, I understand that each person’s experience is unique, making them the experts in their own lives.


At Séimh Psychotherapy therapy is tailored to the individual needs of each adult, adolescent, or child, providing a compassionate and supportive environment where clients can feel safe to explore their thoughts, feelings, and experiences.


The approach is neurodiverse positive, LGBTQIA+ affirming, and trauma informed, meeting every individual where they are at right now.

Services & Fees

The standard fee for an individual 50-minute session is €70.


I strongly believe in social justice and equality and am committed to making therapy accessible to all. A sliding scale is available for individuals experiencing financial difficulty.


Assessment and review sessions with parents and carers of under 18s attending therapy are 60-minute sessions and incur a fee of €80.
