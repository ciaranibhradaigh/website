About Me

Hi, I'm Áine.


I believe there is no “one-size-fits-all” approach to therapy. Every individual - child, adolescent, or adult - is unique and brings their own lived experience, strengths, and challenges. Therapy should feel collaborative, flexible, and tailored to the individual needs and goals of each client.


I work integratively, drawing from a range of evidence-based therapeutic approaches to provide personalised support. These approaches may include:


  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Gestalt Therapy
  • Narrative Therapy
  • Person-Centred Therapy
  • Attachment-Based Approaches
  • Mindfulness
  • Trauma-Informed Practice
  • Creative Therapies
  • Integrative Play Therapy for Children

By adapting therapy to each individual client, my goal is to create a supportive and meaningful therapeutic experience that respects each client’s emotional, developmental, and psychological needs.


At the heart of my work is the evidence-based belief that the therapeutic relationship itself is central to creating lasting and meaningful change. I work collaboratively with adults, adolescents, children, and families to ensure therapy feels safe, empowering, and supportive. Clients are encouraged to have an active voice in their therapeutic journey, helping shape sessions in ways that feel comfortable, engaging, and beneficial for them. My approach is neurodiverse positive, LGBTQIA+ affirming, and trauma informed.


Whether you are seeking support for yourself, your teenager, or your child, therapy can offer a space to feel understood, develop healthier coping strategies, and build emotional resilience.


If you would like to know more about the way I work, please get in touch to arrange a free 30-minute consultation.

What I Offer

Adult Counselling & Psychotherapy

My work with adults focuses on both present-day challenges and the ways earlier life experiences can continue to shape thoughts, emotions, relationships, and behaviours.

I support adults experiencing:

  • Anxiety and depression
  • Perinatal and post-partum mental health difficulties
  • Intergenerational trauma
  • Family estrangement
  • Domestic violence and coercive control
  • Sexual assault and abuse
  • Addiction
  • Chronic illness
  • Feelings of being stuck or overwhelmed
  • Stress, burnout, and emotional difficulties

Therapy offers a confidential space to explore experiences, gain insight, and develop healthier ways of coping and relating.

Adolescent & Teen Therapy

A safe and supportive therapeutic space for teenagers experiencing school stress, anxiety, bullying, low self-esteem, emotional regulation difficulties, and identity challenges.

Services & Fees

The standard fee for an individual 50-minute session is €70.


I strongly believe in social justice and equality and am committed to making therapy accessible to all. A sliding scale is available for individuals experiencing financial difficulty. If cost feels like a barrier, please don’t hesitate to get in touch — we can discuss a fee arrangement that works for your circumstances. The goal at Séimh Psychotherapy is to provide compassionate, accessible support regardless of financial situation.


Assessment and review sessions with parents and carers of under 18s attending therapy are 60-minute sessions and incur a fee of €80.
